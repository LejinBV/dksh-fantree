Our Healthcare Malaysia #DKSHHeroes

We know no bounds when it comes to delivering life-saving medicines. When flights and ferries to the island of Langkawi were suspended during the COVID-19 pandemic, effectively closing the island off, DKSH Malaysia chartered speed boats to deliver medicines that have only 48 hours’ delivery shelf life to Hospital Langkawi. Truly, this is our entrepreneurial spirit in action.
